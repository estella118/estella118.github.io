OUR PURPOSE

i . n . s . p . i . r . e .

Estella Chen and Katarina Wu are the creators of the website. They decided to create the website when they took the time to observe middle school students. The results? They decided what the majority of the middle school students lacked were inspiration. This website is meant to serve as an inspirational site where middle schoolers can seek motivation and inspiration. Not only does this website offer new ideas and motivate students, it also has gorgeous wallpapers with quotes on them. There are four categories of wallpapers. Motivation. Friendship. Self-worth. Bullying. The creators put together a lot of time and dedication to help students in middle school. They hope through the website, students will find comfort and feel at ease.
